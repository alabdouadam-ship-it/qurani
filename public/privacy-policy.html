Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2025-11-13

Who we are

Qurani is a Quran app that respects your privacy and aims for local-first functionality.

What we collect

  • Installation ID (random UUID, per installation)
  • App info (name, package, version, build)
  • Platform info (OS/browser basics as applicable)
  • Locale (system language/country and in-app language)
  • Time zone (name and UTC offset)
  • Collected-at timestamp

No hardware device IDs, no advertising IDs, no precise location.

Why we collect

Diagnostics, reliability, and—if enabled later—push notification delivery (using rotating push tokens).

Storage & retention

Stored locally; if server sync is enabled, the same JSON may be sent securely.
